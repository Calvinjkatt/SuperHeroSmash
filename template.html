Rules

These are the official rules of SUPERHERO SMASH for the Player vs Computer version

In this game each round, you must choose one of three superheros to fight by your side. Each Superhero is effective against a certain opponent but has a weakness against another. You must choose wisely or you will lose

  • Superheros
  • The Dark Knight: BATMAN
      • Overpowers: Superman
      Defeated By: Flash
  • The Man of Steel: SUPERMAN
      • Overpowers: Flash
      Defeated By: Batman
  • The Scarlet Speedster: FLASH
      • Overpowers: Batman
      Defeated By: Superman

    Have fun Battling
